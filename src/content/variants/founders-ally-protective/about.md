## About

My name is Christian Naths. I'm a senior software engineer based in Canada, and for the past decade I've been the technical partner for founders who needed someone they could trust with the most important part of their business.

The way I work is different from what you'll find at an agency or on a freelance marketplace. There are no layers between us — no project managers, no junior developers, no hand-offs to people you've never met. The person you talk to is the person who builds. When I take on your project, I'm personally accountable for every decision, every line of code, and every deadline.

The products I've built are still running in production years after launch. One was acquired — and I personally handled the technical due diligence during the transition, protecting the founder's interests throughout the process. These aren't experiments or proofs of concept. They're real products that real businesses depend on, and they've held up because they were built to last.

When a project grows beyond what one person should handle, I hire and manage additional developers. When it doesn't, you have my full, undivided attention. Either way, the accountability stays with me.
