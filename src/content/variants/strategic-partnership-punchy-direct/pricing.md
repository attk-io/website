## You're going to ask what this costs. Here's the honest answer.

### Discovery — $1,500

A paid working session where we figure out if this is the right fit. You walk me through your vision, I tell you what it'll take to build it, and we both decide if we want to work together. If we move forward, this becomes the foundation for everything that follows. If we don't, you walk away with a clear technical picture of what your product needs.

### Base Platform — $15,000 – $35,000

Every product needs a foundation — the infrastructure that isn't unique to your business but is essential for it to function. Depending on what your product requires, this includes:

- A marketing site that represents your brand
- User accounts and authentication
- Billing and payment processing
- In-app notifications
- A blog or content platform
- Deployment, hosting, and monitoring infrastructure

A bare-bones build starts at $15,000. A full-featured foundation runs up to $35,000. We figure out exactly what you need during discovery.

### Your Product — $125/hour

This is the work that makes your product _yours_ — the business logic, the workflows, the features that no template or boilerplate covers. This gets scoped during discovery and built in short cycles so you always know where your investment is going.
