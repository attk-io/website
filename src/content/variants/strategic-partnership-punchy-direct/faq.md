## You Might Be Wondering

**What if the project doesn't work out?**
Every engagement starts with a paid discovery phase. By the time we commit to a full build, we both know exactly what we're building and why. I don't take projects I don't believe in.

**How involved do I need to be?**
You stay in the loop without being in the weeds. I own the technical execution and bring you in for strategic decisions. You'll always know what's happening and why — in plain language.

**How is this different from hiring an agency?**
At an agency, you're a ticket in a queue. Here, I'm personally accountable for your product. No account managers, no hand-offs, no layers between you and the person writing the code.

**Can you handle a bigger build?**
Yes. I've hired and managed teams of developers when the project demanded it. You get the focus of a solo practitioner with the capacity to scale when it matters.

**What if something goes wrong?**
I'll tell you. Proactively, in plain language, with a plan to fix it. No surprises, no spin. That's what you're paying for — someone who treats your product like their own.

**Who owns the code?**
You do. Everything I build is yours — the code, the infrastructure, the documentation. If we part ways, you walk away with a product that any competent developer can pick up.
